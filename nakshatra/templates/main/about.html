About

Nakshatra'20

NAKSHATRA is an all India MCA meet organized by the ambitious students of the MCA department. The two day technical fest is organized under the Computer Science and Engineering Department of NIT Calicut. The responses received last few years were very encouraging and it has motivated us to take a step further and make Nakshatra’20 even bigger than last year’s. This technical meet indulges an array of both technical as well as nontechnical events along with talks by renowned and respected individuals from the IT industry.

Our Mission

We provide a forum of like-minded people to interact, share and discuss the current affairs in the field of Computer Science and help its growth in the future
